    FAQ Section

    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    Here are the answers to some of the questions that we frequently get from our clients.


    How many free hours of support?
    75 hours

    What types of payment are accepted?
    We accept credit card, debit card, net banking, and payments via UPI. There is a wallet option available.

    Are there any self-help videos?
    Yes, our app comes with a step-by-step tutorial, that can be revisited anytime.

    Is multi-device sync available?
    Yes, since our app operates within the cloud, any progress is automatically saved and can be accessed from other devices that comply with the GDPR rules.

    How frequent is the app updated?
    Since our services are cloud based, there is a bi-annual version update that takes place on the 3rd Sunday of September and the 2nd Sunday of March during which, all activities are suspended and the app will be under maintenance. Clients will be notified 2 weeks prior to the maintenance schedule.
